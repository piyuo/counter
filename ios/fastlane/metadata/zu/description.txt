Lolu uhlelo lokusebenza olukwazi ukubona nokubala abantu abahamba ngezinyawo ngokuzenzakalayo, aluhloze ukuxhumana kwe-intanethi futhi lungasetshenziswa ngokushesha ngemva kokufakwa.

Isifinyezo Sezici
- Ukubala abantu ngesikhathi sangempela: Uhlelo luyabona abantu abahamba ngesibuko futhi lubale inani ngokuzenzakalayo.
- Ukusetshenziswa ungaxhumekile: Yonke imisebenzi ingasebenza endaweni ngaphandle kokuxhumana kwe-intanethi.
- Imithombo eminingi yesibuko: Iyasekela amakhamera akahlelwe, ividiyo ehambayo namakhamera angaphandle (ukwesekwa okuhlukile ngokwencikinciki).
- Ukurekhoda kwesikhathi eside: Ingarekhoda ngokuqhubekayo amahora angama-24 futhi ibuze idatha noma isiphi isikhathi.

Indlela Yokusetshenzisa
1. Bheka ikhamera emphakathini noma ekungeneni ofuna ukukulinde.
2. Vula iphrojekthi futhi khethe umthombo wesibuko.
3. Ngemva kokuqala, uhlelo luzobona futhi lubale ngokuzenzakalayo.

Amanothi
- Ukunemba kokubala kuthinteka ngengosi, ukukhanya, izithiyo, njll. Kunconywa ukuthi ulungise indawo yekhamera ngokuya ngezimo zangempela.
- Nakuba kunganembi ngo-100%, ukusebenzisa imodeli ye-AI engaziguquli kunciphisa amaphutha abantu.
- Konke ukucutshungulwa kwedatha kupheliswa endaweni - akukho izithombe noma idatha elayishwa.

Izibonelo Ezifanele
- Ukuqonda ukuhamba kwabantu ezindaweni (njengokungena, imigwaqo, izindawo zokubonisa).
- Ucwaningo, ukuhlela kwemicimbi noma ukubuka kwansuku zonke.
- Kungaziqala ukusetshenziswa ngokushesha ngaphandle kokwengeza amathuluzi e-hardware.
