Ukulungiswa kwamaphutha

- Kulungiswe ukuphela obekwenzeka lapho uvala iphrojekthi ngemva kwamasekhondi angu-30.
- Kuxazululwe inkinga eyayenza ukuthi amanye amadivayisi abonise isithombe esiguqukile kusuka kukhamera yangaphambili.
- Kulungiswe ukuhumusha okuningana okungalungile kwezilimi.
- Kubhekwana nephutha lapho i-app yayizobambelela khona lapho imvume yekhamera ivinjelwa.
